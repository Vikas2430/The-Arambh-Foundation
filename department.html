Departments

Graphics department


The Graphics Department of Aarambh Organization specialises in creating dynamic reels and captivating posters and cover pages on various events. We even prepare the layout of our Instagram page. With a keen eye for design and a commitment to excellence, our team crafts visually stunning assets that effectively communicate the essence and message of each event. By using the latest design tools and techniques, we make sure that our reels and posters are not only visually appealing but also align with the unit'/project work .

Social media department


The Social Media Department focuses on managing the organization's social media presence, aiming to expand its reach and engagement. Currently, we maintain five key platforms: Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, the organization's website, and YouTube. Responsibilities include capturing videos and photos during the drives conducted by the Aarambh organization to share compelling visual content and filtering them which we can use for posts and reel making,to increase the active followers of the organisation on the social media handles,to encourage people to like,comment and share the posts and reels posted on the Instagram and to increase the engagement. Our objective is to amplify the organization's online presence and foster meaningful connections with the audience.

  • Eishi Saharia- Head
  • Shubha Manaswi- Coordinator
  • Saundarya Bhardwaj- Coordinator
  • Satvik Pandey- Charge de Affair

Content department


The content department at the Aarambh Organisation is responsible for documenting the working of this organisation. This includes various drives, donation camps and events conducted by Aarambh. Content team is responsible for writing captions for social media posts, blogs for the website , Memorandum of Understanding and Letters of Recommendation, in english and hindi. The department works closely with other departments, i.e., with social media,graphics and marketing to ensure that the content aligns with the goals and targets of the organisation. The content department is the team of seasoned, passionate and proficient content writers.

Marketing department


The primary objective of the marketing department at Aarambh is to secure funding and pursue various collaboration prospects. Recently, we engaged in brainstorming sessions to generate ideas for fundraising and partnerships, documented in an Excel sheet for future reference. Additionally, we compiled a comprehensive database of schools and colleges in Delhi. This database will facilitate outreach to institutions capable of supplying books, aiding in the establishment of an Aarambh library.

  • Praveer Mishra- Head
  • Shivani Sharma - Coordinator

Event department


An event department within the Aarambh is responsible for planning, organizing, and executing various events. Recently we have organised the MAYANAGRI. The department typically consists of event planners, coordinators, charge D' affairs and supervisor who work together to ensure the success of each event. There are majorly two events during a period of one year , one of which is our foundation day (SURAMYA) and one is a fund raiser event. We as an event department are responsible for planning and organizing freshers to farewell.

Key responsibilities of an event department include:

  • Event planning
  • Logistics Management
  • On-Site Event Management
  • Post-Event Evaluation

Hr department


The HR department plays a crucial role in creating a safe and conducive environment for members to work at their maximum potential. Primarily focused on recruitment and maintaining a satisfied workforce, HR also oversees the data management of members, evaluates their performance, and takes necessary actions to enhance their experience within the organization. Ensuring a safe working environment is paramount, reflecting the department's commitment to employee well-being and organizational success.
