Suramya

Foundation Day is a momentous occasion that marks the inception of an organisation, institution, or community. Suramya which means 'beautiful' in every aspect is an annual event organised to celebrate the foundation day of The Aarambh Organization, which is also the day we got recognition and were registered by the Niti Aayog..

Dinamica

Dinamica

The Aarambh organises Dinamica to cheer up the Freshers and have them interact with our Aarambh family. It welcomes the freshers with a classic freshers party which allows the new family members to interact with each other. It allows everyone to celebrate the start of something amazing. All the units join in together with their prepared performances of various sorts. This celebration is a chance for incoming members to forge friendships, create lasting memories, and embark on a journey of personal and professional growth. All of this is paired with loads of games and competitions

Aatishbaazi

Aatishbaazi

This vibrant and enchanting event marks the beginning of Diwali, one of the most cherished festivals among the Indian diaspora worldwide. This Diwali mela is a feast for the senses, a kaleidoscope of colours, flavours, and traditions that ignite the spirit of unity and joy. It is one of the biggest Diwali celebrations at Delhi University. The mela has a wide variety of food stalls, games, music, and a lot of dancing.
