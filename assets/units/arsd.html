Atma Ram Sanatan dharma college

ARSD

Cause:- cleanliness and sanitation

Summary

Currently, the ARSD unit boasts a team of 50+ members, all of whom contribute dedicated efforts toward promoting cleanliness and sanitation within the community. Through their impactful initiatives including awareness campaigns, waste management programs, and active participation in cleanliness drives, they strive to foster a healthier and more hygienic environment for all.

The unit's targets include collaborating with the MCD, conducting health camps, installing dustbins, and promoting a healthier and more hygienic lifestyle for the people of the slum areas.
